# Geometry Discovery Project


## Description
The [Geometry Discovery Project](https://gdp.math.uci.edu/) is a comprehensive online platform dedicated to advancing the understanding of Differential Geometry. Founded by a collaborative team of students from the University of California, Irvine and renowned Differential Geometry professor Zhiqin Lu, the project aims to make the intricate concepts of this mathematical field more accessible to a broader audience.

This website is designed to cater to those with a keen interest in Differential Geometry, offering an extensive collection of topics that are fundamental to the discipline. Each topic is meticulously covered through detailed articles that not only present rigorous mathematical proofs but also integrate interactive diagrams to enhance comprehension. These visualizations play a crucial role in helping users grasp the complex geometric structures and relationships that are central to Differential Geometry.

As part of the project, I focused on creating various visualizations using advanced mathematical and geometrical methods. These visual tools are specifically designed to complement the theoretical content, providing users with an intuitive understanding of the topics discussed. By manipulating these diagrams, users can explore the geometrical properties and theorems in a dynamic and engaging way, bridging the gap between abstract mathematical concepts and their practical applications.

The Geometry Discovery Project stands as a valuable resource for students, educators, and enthusiasts alike, fostering a deeper appreciation of Differential Geometry through the seamless integration of theoretical rigor and interactive learning.
