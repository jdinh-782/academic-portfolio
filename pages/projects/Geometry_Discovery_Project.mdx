## Description
The [Geometry Discovery Project](https://gdp.math.uci.edu/) is a functional website founded by the students of UCI and Differential Geometry professor, Zhiqin Lu. Together, we formed this project to provide those that are intersted in Differential 
Geometry with the necessary topics and information that are important to the field. Each topic is covered with an article of proofs and an interactive diagram to help visualize the topic in further detail. I spent my time creating various visualizations
using mathematical and geometrical methods. <br/>
[Source](https://github.com/jdinh-782/Geometry-Discovery-Project-Website)
